One Up

Advanced Propulsion Systems

Develop a mission to test next-generation propulsion technologies, such as ion drives or nuclear thermal engines, aimed at reducing travel time to distant planets. This mission will evaluate the efficiency and performance of these advanced systems in space, focusing on their ability to provide higher thrust and fuel efficiency. By conducting in-space demonstrations, the mission will gather crucial data to inform future spacecraft designs, ultimately enhancing our capability for faster crewed and uncrewed missions to destinations like Mars and beyond.

Deorbiting Small Satellites

Develop and test technology for small satellites that can actively deorbit themselves at the end of their operational life. This initiative will explore solutions like drag sails and propulsion systems to safely lower orbits and facilitate re-entry. By integrating these technologies, satellites can minimize space debris and ensure responsible end-of-life management. In-orbit demonstrations will assess the effectiveness of these methods, promoting sustainable practices in satellite design and contributing to a cleaner orbital environment.
