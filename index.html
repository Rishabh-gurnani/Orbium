Team APJN presents 'Orbium'

Our project tackles space debris using a "one up, one down" approach. We launch a rocket into low Earth orbit, where the deployed satellite employs a Hohmann transfer to capture defunct satellites contributing to debris. After securing the defunct satellite, it performs a deorbiting maneuver to safely return the debris to Earth. This process effectively places a new satellite in orbit while reducing space debris, promoting a safer space environment.
